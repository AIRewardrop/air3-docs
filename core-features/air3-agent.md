# AIR3 Agent

The heart of the project is the **AIR3 Agent**, an AI-powered entity designed to:

* **Analyze Trends**
  * Scan thousands of tweets, on-chain data, and market movements in real time.
  * Expand feed scraping from 5 selected profiles to up to **10,000 tweets/day**, capturing a wide range of market sentiment and alpha.
* **Engage Socially**
  * Autonomously post on Twitter/X, Telegram, Discord, and TikTok, reacting to tags and DMs with relevant context.
  * Provide voice (text-to-speech) responses and even video interactions featuring **3D AI Avatars** (Metahuman on Unreal Engine 5).
* **Support DeFi**
  * Simplify interactions with liquidity pools, yield farming, or staking protocols.
  * Offer real-time market insights, direct trading tools, and volume updates from platforms like **Dexscreener**, **Cookie.fun**, **VVAIFU.fun**, and **Coingecko Terminal**.
* **Livestream & 3D Avatars**
  * Present 24/7 streams of an **avatar** controlled by the AI Agent on YouTube, Twitch, and X (formerly Twitter).
  * Interact with the audience in real time, combining advanced text, voice, and animated facial expressions.
