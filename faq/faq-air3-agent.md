# AIR3 Agent FAQ

## What is the AIR3 Agent?

AIR3 is an always-on AI agent experience operated by AIRewardrop, available across social and community channels. It provides market-oriented responses, charts, and AI-assisted analysis workflows.

## Where is the AIR3 text agent available by default?

The AIR3 text agent is available by default in the official AIR3 Discord and Telegram communities.

## What is AIRTool?

AIRTool is the product module that allows third-party projects to rent the AIR3 text agent for their own Discord and/or Telegram communities through a buy-and-burn activation model.

## What is AIRSponsor?

AIRSponsor allows projects to purchase sponsor visibility using a buy-and-burn flow. Sponsor slots can place the brand on-screen while AIR3 delivers a scripted call-out live.

## What is AIRSocial?

AIRSocial covers social engagement mechanics, campaigns, and utility-linked community interactions within the broader AIR3 ecosystem.

## Are AIRTool and AIRSponsor part of AIRTrading?

No. They are ecosystem utility products. AIRTrading is the execution product. AIRTool and AIRSponsor are service and visibility modules.

## Why is AIR3 token utility connected to these modules?

The objective is to connect real product usage to token demand and burn activity, rather than relying only on speculative utility use cases.
