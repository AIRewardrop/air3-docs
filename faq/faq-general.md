# General FAQ

## What is AIR3?

AIR3 is the flagship AI agent and product ecosystem operated by AIRewardrop. It combines live AI-driven market intelligence, public trade traceability, product utilities, and token-linked services.

## What is the difference between AIRTrack and AIRTrading?

AIRTrack is a live tracking and simulation layer used to test and validate strategies in public. It records entries, exits, and simulated PnL for transparency and strategy assessment.

AIRTrading is the real execution environment. Only strategies that pass live validation on AIRTrack are eligible for deployment into AIRTrading.

## Where can I verify AIR3 activity publicly?

AIR3 activity can be verified through multiple public surfaces:
- X posts from [@AIRewardrop](https://x.com/AIRewardrop) for trade opens and close confirmations
- AIRdApp and AIRTrack interfaces for position history and PnL views
- On-chain records and protocol events where applicable

## Is AIR3 only a trading product?

No. AIR3 is an ecosystem. It includes an AI agent layer, tracking/simulation tools, execution products, community features, and token-linked utilities such as AIRSponsor and AIRTool.

## Is AIRTrack the same as a real trading vault?

No. AIRTrack does not execute real trades. It is a tracking, simulation, and validation environment.
