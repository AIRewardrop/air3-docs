# AIRSponsor

AIRSponsor is the AIR3 live visibility product powered by a buy + burn access flow.

## What AIRSponsor does

Projects can use AIRSponsor to place their brand inside the AIR3 live spotlight.

Typical flow:
1. Buy AIR3
2. Burn the required amount
3. Submit sponsor brief / assets / script
4. Go live once verified

## AIRSponsor value proposition

AIRSponsor is designed to let projects:
- appear on-screen in AIR3 live sessions
- receive a scripted sponsor call-out delivered by AIR3 in real time
- gain exposure across AIR3 live content surfaces

## Why it matters for AIR3 utility

AIRSponsor links utility to visibility:
- AIR3 is used for access
- burn action reduces supply
- sponsor inventory ties token usage to product demand
