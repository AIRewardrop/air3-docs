# Token Utility by Product

This page maps AIR3 utility to the current product stack.

## AIR3 Agent
- ecosystem identity and utility anchor
- public interface driving product discovery and usage

## AIRTrack
- primarily a tracking/simulation and transparency layer
- strategy validation stage before execution promotion
- not a direct user-fund execution utility sink

## AIRTrading
- execution and profit distribution layer
- buyback and user rewards integration
- protocol-level utility linkage when profitable

## AIRSocial
- community participation layer
- can support future utility unlocks, points, and campaign mechanics

## AIRSponsor
- buy + burn sponsor slot unlock
- visibility utility tied to AIR3 live media presence

## AIRTool
- buy + burn to unlock AIR3 agent deployment in third-party Telegram/Discord communities
- direct service utility

## White-label and partner services (ecosystem level)
- AIR3 can also be part of broader ecosystem value capture through AIRewardrop services and partner deployments
