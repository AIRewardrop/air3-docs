# Tokenomics Overview

This section documents the AIR3 token reference data, utility design, liquidity venues, and public tracking links.

The tokenomics model is designed to connect product usage, protocol activity, and transparency. Real-time metrics are sourced from public explorers and market dashboards and may change continuously.

## Scope of this section

- Token identifiers and contract addresses
- Liquidity venues and trading links
- Public supply and burn tracking references
- Protocol-related treasury and contract addresses
- Utility and value-flow context across AIR3 products
